# Readability Formulas & Scoring

## Flesch Reading Ease

```
Score = 206.835 - 1.015 × (total words / total sentences) - 84.6 × (total syllables / total words)
```

| Score | Difficulty | Grade Level |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 90-100 | Very Easy | 5th grade |
| 80-89 | Easy | 6th grade |
| 70-79 | Fairly Easy | 7th grade |
| 60-69 | Standard | 8th-9th grade |
| 50-59 | Fairly Difficult | 10th-12th grade |
| 30-49 | Difficult | College |
| 0-29 | Very Difficult | Graduate |

**SEO target: 60-70** (Standard to Fairly Easy)

## Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level

```
Grade = 0.39 × (total words / total sentences) + 11.8 × (total syllables / total words) - 15.59
```

**SEO target: 8-10** (accessible to broad audiences)

## Gunning Fog Index

```
Fog = 0.4 × [(words / sentences) + 100 × (complex words / words)]
```

Complex words = words with 3+ syllables (excluding common suffixes like -ed, -es, -ing)

**Target: 8-12** (readable by high school students)

## Content Metrics Targets

| Metric | Target | Warning | Critical |
|--------|--------|---------|----------|
| Avg sentence length | 15-20 words | 20-25 words | 25+ words |
| Avg paragraph length | 2-4 sentences | 5 sentences | 6+ sentences |
| Passive voice ratio | <20% | 20-30% | 30%+ |
| Complex word % | <15% | 15-20% | 20%+ |
| Transition words | 25-35% of sentences | <20% | <10% |
| Subheading frequency | Every 300-400 words | Every 500 words | 600+ words |

## Sentence Variety Analysis

Good content mixes sentence lengths:
- **Short** (5-10 words): Punchy. Creates emphasis. Breaks rhythm.
- **Medium** (11-20 words): The workhorse of good content, carrying most information.
- **Long** (21-30 words): Used sparingly, these sentences add nuance and complexity when the topic demands deeper explanation.

**Ideal distribution**: 20% short, 60% medium, 20% long
**Red flag**: More than 3 consecutive sentences of similar length

## Practical Optimization Steps

1. **Run readability check** on the full content
2. **Break long sentences** (25+ words) into two at natural conjunction points
3. **Split long paragraphs** (5+ sentences) at topic shifts
4. **Replace complex words** with simpler alternatives where meaning is preserved
5. **Add transition words** at paragraph and section beginnings
6. **Convert passive voice** to active where the actor is known
7. **Re-check scores** to confirm improvement
